01 — About

A bit about me.

I'm a Software Engineer at Goldman Sachs (Global Banking & Markets), where I work on engineering systems that power financial markets. My background spans financial engineering, data analysis, cybersecurity, and AI/ML research.

Before Goldman, I worked as an Application & Database Security Analyst at Fidelity Bank Nigeria safeguarding digital assets, and as a Data Analyst at Credit Direct translating complex datasets into strategic insights. My career began at the University of Lagos, where I served as a Robotics Lab Assistant and honed my skills in ML and software engineering.

I hold a B.Sc. in Systems Engineering from the University of Lagos, and I also completed my M.S. in Computer Science & Quantitative Methods at Austin Peay State University with a focus on Predictive Analytics. My research interests include AI-driven trading systems, software supply chain security, and data privacy in critical infrastructure.
